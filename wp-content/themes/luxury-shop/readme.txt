== Description ==

The Luxury Shop theme is designed exclusively for brands that want to deliver an elevated online shopping experience. Whether you run a boutique, designer store, jewelry shop, online boutique, luxury items, premium goods, fashion ecommerce, cosmetics brand, or luxury accessories collection, this theme helps you showcase products with elegance and clarity. Its refined layout, modern typography, and polished color palette combine to create the perfect ambiance for any luxury shop looking to attract premium customers. With responsive design and seamless compatibility with WooCommerce, your high-end products will always look stunning across all devices. This theme empowers luxury retailers to present their catalog with a modern, high-class feel that reflects exclusivity and style. Built with speed, UX, and performance in mind, the Luxury Shop theme includes customizable sections for featured collections, trending products, premium offers, and seasonal highlights. Store owners can adjust layouts effortlessly using the WordPress Customizer, ensuring every detail aligns with their brand identity. Optimized for SEO and designed using clean, lightweight code, the theme guarantees a smooth browsing experience that keeps customers engaged. Whether your business focuses on luxury fashion, luxury footwear, handbags, watches, perfumes, or designer apparel, this theme enhances product visibility and gives your luxury shop the competitive edge it needs. Perfect for high-end boutiques, elite fashion brands, and exclusive product lines, the theme offers support for stylish product grids, luxury banners, and immersive visuals. The Luxury Shop theme transforms standard eCommerce websites into premium digital storefronts. It’s ideal for luxury gift shops, premium lifestyle brands, upscale marketplaces, and any retailer that values sophistication. With its elegant structure, powerful features, and refined presentation, the Luxury Shop theme ensures your store truly represents the essence of luxury, exclusivity, and premium shopping. 

== Installation ==
        
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the luxury-shop theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away
